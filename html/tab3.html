Axis Inclination

It occurs when the axis of rotation of a planet is tilted relatively towards the plane of its revolution around a larger object, in other words, it is when a planet is tilted as it rotates around the Sun.

The Earth is inclined about 23.5 degrees, this means that sometimes the northern hemisphere is inclined towards the Sun, and others, in the opposite direction.
The inclination of the planet is what gives rise to the seasons, if the earth did not have this inclination, we would not have seasons and day and night would have the same duration all year round.

If the planets rotated with the same axis of inclination of the earth, they would have difficulties in terms of the seasons, since each one rotates at a speed that depends on its distance from the sun, that is, the further away they are from the sun the slower they rotate and vice versa.
